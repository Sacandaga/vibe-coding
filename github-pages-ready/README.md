## Features

- **6 predictions** with expandable reasoning and probability estimates
- **Interactive scenario model** — 4 sliders modeling oil prices, supply loss, GDP impact, and EU recession risk in real time
- **8-week probability forecast** — Chart.js visualization of escalation trajectories
- **Oil price scenario chart** — actual trajectory with 3 forward-looking forecasts
- **Cost analysis** — US vs. Houthi monthly costs showing the 60:1 asymmetry
- **Target categories & equipment** — platforms, strike packages, and estimated costs
- **Recommendations** — prioritized actions with full analytical reasoning

## About

This dashboard is part of a national security simulation project covering U.S. decision-making during the 2025–2026 Houthi Red Sea crisis and the 2026 Iran War. The simulation centers on NSC principals-level decision-making, with documents written from the perspectives of the CJCS, CIA Director, and independent analysts.

All analysis is derived from publicly available sources. This is an academic/educational exercise.
